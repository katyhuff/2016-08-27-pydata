Science

  • builds and organizes knowledge
  • tests explanations about the universe
  • systematically,
  • objectively,
  • transparently,
  • and reproducibly.

Otherwise it's not science.

Computers

should...

  • improve efficiency,
  • reduce human error,
  • automate the mundane,
  • simplify the complex,
  • and accelerate research.

But scientists don't use them effectively.

Software Carpentry Foundation

  • NumFOCUS umbrella
  • Elected Steering Committee
  • Representative, Expert Advisory Council

We make rearchers in science, engineering, and medicine more productive by teaching them basic lab skills for scientific computing.
